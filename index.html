Non-Surgical Height & Presence Program

Follow daily exercises, stretches, and confidence drills to stand taller and move with presence.

Week 1: Daily Routine

Morning Posture & Stretching (5–10 min)

  • Chin tucks: 10 reps
  • Shoulder blade squeezes: 10 reps
  • Hip flexor stretch: 30 sec each side
  • Chest opener stretch: 30 sec

Core & Leg Strength (10–15 min)

  • Plank: 30–60 sec
  • Bird dog: 10 reps per side
  • Bodyweight squats: 15 reps
  • Calf raises: 20 reps

Confidence & Presence (5 min)

  • Mirror walk practice: shoulders back, chest open, chin slightly tucked
  • Eye contact drill: steady gaze for 30 sec

Evening Mobility & Recovery (5–10 min)

  • Hamstring stretches: 30 sec each leg
  • Quad stretches: 30 sec each leg
  • Calf stretches: 30 sec each leg
